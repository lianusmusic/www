IT WILL HAPPEN TO YOU

I composed this tune thinking in a melodic side of the Chromatic approach and using more intervalic ideas. The lines are more extended with not too many eight notes. As you can here, this tune could be more "cantabile" so if you are trying to create your own composition thinking on being melodic but still using the chromaticism , this is a good example of that. I hope you enjoy this
